Social Media Awareness

Use Social Media Wisely and Responsibly

Why Social Media Awareness Matters

Social media helps us communicate, learn, and share ideas. However, it is important to use it responsibly to stay safe and avoid spreading false information.

Be Safe Online

  • Think before you post
  • Keep personal information private
  • Be respectful and kind online

Stop Fake News

  • Check reliable sources
  • Do not share unverified posts
  • Report false information

Use Social Media for Good

Support positive causes, share inspiring stories, and use social media to help others.

Share Smart. Share Safe. Make a Difference.
